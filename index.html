SMS Sender Identifier

Know More about SMS Sender ID format

The SMS Sender ID format follows the pattern XY-ABCDEF:

  • X: Denotes the Telecom Service Provider (TSP)
  • Y: Denotes the License Service Area (LSA)
  • ABCDEF: Is the header assigned to the Principal Entity

Currently, the header length is fixed at six alphanumeric characters, with a total of nine characters when including the prefix (XY) and separator (-). However, regulations allow for headers to be up to 11 characters in total length.

For example, in VM-SBICR, "V" represents Vodafone Idea Ltd., "M" represents the Mumbai service area, and "SBICR" is the header assigned to the Principal Entity.
